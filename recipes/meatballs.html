Meatballs Recipe

Descripton

A true Italian Classic. This recipe is good on top of pasta, or served with a side of fresh baguette.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Panko
  • Eggs
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. First you are going to combine the eggs, ground beef, italian seasoning, and panko into a bowl.
  2. Combine until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed
  3. Shape into small balls
  4. Place into cast iron pan and top with marinara sauce
  5. Put into preheated oven and cook for 35 minutes
  6. Remove and eat hot
  7. Bon Appetit
